### **DOs and DON'Ts**

---

### **DOs** ‚úÖ

‚úÖ **Use High-Quality Images**
- Minimum 1080p, preferably 1440p+
- Professional lighting preferred
- Clear, sharp focus

‚úÖ **Choose Motion That Makes Sense**
- Pan across landscapes
- Zoom in on important details
- Rotate products 360¬∞

‚úÖ **Add Depth to Images**
- Include foreground, middle, and background
- Helps AI create parallax
- More 3D feel

‚úÖ **Match Motion to Your Story**
- Slow = luxury, quality
- Fast = energy, excitement
- Zoom in = focus, importance

‚úÖ **Test Multiple Motions**
- Same image, different types
- A/B test on social media

‚úÖ **Optimize for Platform**
- Instagram: Square (1:1) or Vertical (9:16)
- TikTok: Vertical (9:16)
- YouTube: Horizontal (16:9)

‚úÖ **Use Batch Processing**
- 10 photos ‚Üí 10 videos in 1 hour

---

### **DON'Ts** ‚ùå

‚ùå **Use Low-Quality Images**
- Blurry photos
- Poor lighting
- Pixelated screenshots

‚ùå **Confusing Compositions**
- Cluttered backgrounds
- Multiple focal points
- Unclear main subject

‚ùå **Unrealistic Motion**
- Don't expect objects to move that aren't there
- AI can't add people/characters that don't exist

‚ùå **Extreme Zoom**
- Zooming too close = pixelation
- Sweet spot: 20-40% zoom

‚ùå **Too Much Duration**
- Avoid 60+ second videos from single image
- Optimal: 8-20 seconds

‚ùå **Wrong Image for Motion Type**
- Flat product photo + parallax = awkward
- Match image composition to motion

‚ùå **Ignoring Platform Specs**
- Uploading 16:9 to Instagram = awkward crop
- Always format for platform

---

### **Common Mistakes & Fixes**

**Problem:** Motion feels robotic  
**Fix:** Add "ease in/ease out", choose "organic" motion settings

**Problem:** Lighting shifts during motion  
**Fix:** Start with evenly lit source image

**Problem:** Edges look weird  
**Fix:** Ensure image has enough margin/padding

**Problem:** Motion doesn't match audio  
**Fix:** Plan audio timing first, generate video to match

### **13. Deep Focus**

**Description:**  
Everything in frame, from foreground to background, remains sharply in focus

**Purpose:**

- Emphasize complexity
- Allow viewer to explore entire frame

### **14. Shallow Depth of Field**

**Description:**  
Subject in sharp focus while background blurs (bokeh effect)

**Purpose:**
- Isolate subject
- Create intimacy
- Draw attention to specific element

```

### Mastering SORA Prompts for AI Video Creation

SORA (by OpenAI) offers powerful text-to-video and image-to-video capabilities.

### **Key Guidelines for SORA Prompting**

---

### **1. Start with Detailed Descriptions**

**Include:**

**Characters:**
- Physical appearance
- Clothing and style
- Age and features

**Setting:**
- Environment details
- Decor or weather
- Time of day

**Mood and Style:**
- Emotional atmosphere
- Artistic direction

**Camera Movements:**
- Specific angles
- Movement techniques

**Shot Types:**
- Wide shots
- Close-ups
- POV shots

**Example:**
```
"A cinematic shot of 35-year-old knight in full metal armor 
scaling snowy mountain peak at dawn. Golden sunlight reflects 
off snow, creating dramatic lighting. Epic fantasy tone with 
wide-angle sweeping shot."
```

---

### **2. Strike Balance Between Brevity and Detail**

**Brief Prompts:**
- Allow SORA to interpret
- Good for exploration

Example:
```
"A magical forest at twilight"
```

**Detailed Prompts:**
- Provide clear guidance
- Better for specific visions

Example:
```
"An astronaut exploring red desert planet under soft pink sky, 
captured with wide-angle lens, dramatic shadows from two suns, 
alien rock formations in background, sense of isolation and wonder"
```

---

### **3. Stay Grounded in Realistic Concepts**

**Avoid:**
```
"A dragon exploding into fireworks made of water"
```

**Better:**
```
"A dragon flying over moonlit forest, its scales glistening 
in silver light"
```

**Guidelines:**
- Use scenarios that can be rendered believably
- Avoid impossible transformations
- Keep physics plausible

---

### **4. Utilize Emotional and Lighting Cues**

**Lighting Conveys Mood:**

**Golden Hour:** Warm, nostalgic, romantic  
**Blue Hour:** Mysterious, contemplative, cool  
**Harsh Sunlight:** Intense, dramatic  
**Soft Overcast:** Calm, subdued  

**Example:**
```
"A tranquil lake surrounded by glowing autumn foliage at sunrise, 
warm golden light casting long reflections on still water, evoking 
serenity and peaceful reflection"
```

---

### **5. Use Cinematic Language**

**Camera Angles:**
- "Over-the-shoulder shot"
- "Bird's-eye view"
- "Low-angle looking up"

**Lens Types:**
- "Wide-angle lens (24mm)"
- "50mm prime lens"
- "Telephoto (85mm)"

**Techniques:**
- "Tracking shot following subject"
- "Soft focus on background"
- "Dramatic backlighting"

**Example:**
```
"Protagonist silhouetted against fiery sunset, captured with 
telephoto lens compressing background, camera slowly zooms in 
creating dramatic tension, warm rim lighting on character's edges"
```

---

### **6. Experiment with Artistic Styles**

**Examples:**

**Claymation:**
```
"Claymation style stop-motion, handcrafted clay characters 
with visible fingerprints, whimsical movements"
```

**Black-and-White Noir:**
```
"Black and white film noir, high contrast lighting, deep shadows, 
1940s aesthetic, detective walking through foggy alley"
```

**Cyberpunk Neon:**
```
"Cyberpunk neon aesthetic, vibrant pink and blue lighting, 
futuristic city with holographic advertisements"
